# Hethadenee waunauyaunee vadan Luke vanenana
# Michael White Hawk (tr.)
# Arapaho (NR)
# https://www.gutenberg.org/files/30102/30102-0.txt

Hanau waunauthathee hadnedanuu hejadenenau hadjenanuu hauthase
vavesee hauthedau hena hayauhuhau nanāe hājavaānethauwuthajauthaunin,

Hasevenānauau nanaa hehethee jasesee vasesā hāenuu nau nejaanuu
hanadedaunau;

Hasethajaunau hethadee heee nananenau jea, vavesethaugudenau
vahee hayauhuhau hehetheejasesee hadnewauthaunauwunathane nedānee,
javaānethadee Theauthenus,

Hadeaenauwu hethauwuu hena hayauhuhau nesechauhauthehane.

Hena hese Herod, naja Judea, jasaa vevethahehe Zacharias haseede,
hadauchunanede Abia: nau henene hedaunāhene Aarauneau, nau heneseedaunin
Elisabeth.

Nau haenesauuthāhenauau hathaāhenith Hejavaneauthu, thaunaugudethee
vahee nanavaavedaunau nau hauchuwudaunau Vahadāde dejauthauwuvade.

Nau hehauwunesaunenauau, hanau henee hehauwunesaune Elisabeth; nau
wauwu haenesauu vaehahenenauau.

Nau hethauwuu, henee daunausuvadanedaude hathaāhenith Hejavaneauthu
hāenauauchaude,

Nesedaunethe vevethahehehau, nesedaude hadnejaāde yehauthe
hevadannauauwunene Vahadāhenith.

Nau haunauude henanedanede haevevethahedaune jathee nauauchuu jaa.

Nau haenauhauva hautheaune Vahadāde haetheaugune hejavese jaanaa.

Nau hāenauhauwaude Zacharias, haejanethajau, nau nāthajaude
haeedasaa.

Hau nuu hautheaune, jevanāthajau hathāhuk, Zacharias: havevethahede
nanedauwuhuu; nau hadasea Elisabeth hadjanesewaude hāau, nau Jaun
hadeseau.

Nau nananin hadaunauwuthajau nau hadeneethajau; nau hadnewauthathee
neethajauthee hāejanesee.

Hanau haudvasāthaa hathaāhenith Vahadāhenith, nau haudnehauwuvane
daudause dadajevenenaje, wauāthe neauthunaje; nau haudnechauchaunaudede
Hevadanauwauthauwunene Hejavaneauthu, haa hehethee hāejanesee.

Nau haudnewauthaa dāeyaunenede Israel hadjaechuwauyāaunaude hesee
Vahadāhenith Henesaunaunenau.

Nau haudnedauwaunaude nauguu Vadanauwauthauwu nau neesenaudāhenith
Elias hadjaechuwauyāauau vadauhau henesaunaunenau hesee dāeyaunauauau nau
nejenedauvathee gaugauuthajaudenau hadauethadee; hadnesaanudede
henanedanede hesanaa hesee Vahadāhenith.

Nau Zacharias naudedusaene nuu? wauwu vanaehahenenau nau nadasea wauwu
vadavehahenede, hathauhuk hautheaune.

Nau hautheaune hanaāauchuhananadethāde, Nananenau Gabriel henee
theaugudaunauau hathaāhede Hejavaneauthau; nau janeautheavānau
haudnāedauwunathana, nau hadnauhauthehathana nuu neethajaude waunauyauau.

Nau, naune, nananin haudnejanadene nau haudnehauwuneanade,
haunaude hese nuu hayauhuhau nauauchaugu hethauwuu, hanau nananin
daujethauwaudauwu nadanadede, nuu hadnethauwunauauchuu nauauchaugaune.

Nau henanedanede haedauyauhauvanauau Zacharias, nau haegaugauanadauwu
henee dauguthetheaugude vadannauauwuu.

Nau hāejenauāhede, haejeneanadetha: nau haeaenauwu daunauhauthāde
vadannauauwuu: haevathasauwuna nau haejanade.

Nau hethauwuu, henee, chauchaunu hese hāesevadanedaude haeyehau hesee
nehayau hedauauwuu.

Nau heee hena hese henene Elisabeth hanaāenesaunenith, nau haegauduse
nehayau hayauthaunenethe hesese heee, hanaāenehede,

Hesau Vahadāde naasehānau hesee hāedaugauhauvānau hadgudanauwunānau
nāedayāhede henanedaa.

Nau nanedaudauchuauwaude hesese hautheaun Gabriel haejeautheawau
hehethee Hejavaneauthu hesee hedan Gananee haseenaugu Nazareth,

Hesāhedāeyaun haeenenehauwaune henanin Joseph haenaneeseenith,
hehethee hedauauwuu David; nau hesāhedāeyaun Mare haenaaseede.

Nau hautheaun haeedasaa, nau hanaāenehethāde, Haunauwuthajau, nananin
vechauthane, Vahadāde hanedaudaunāne: hauwunaunane hesānaa.

Nau hāenauhauwaude, haejanethajaude hedanadedaunin, nau haegaugauanade
daun hadnesenaunenechuhude nuu hadnesauau.

Nau hautheaun, Jevanāthajau, hathāhuk, Mare: hanau nananin vechauthāne
Hejavaneauthau.

Nau, naune, nananin hadnenesaunin, nau hadneahene, nau JESUS hadeseau.

Hadvasāthaa, nau Vāā Hejavaa hadethauva: nau Vahadāde Hejavaneauthau
hadvenaude naja theaugudau henesaunaune David:

Nau hadnajawaudauau hededanin Jacob jethauujenee; nau henajanede
hadnehauwuthauuvadauaune.

Hanaāenehethaude Mare nuu hautheaune, Haddusenesau nuu hanau
nāhauwāenaunau henane?

Nau hautheaun hanaāauchuhananadethāde, Hejavaneauthau
Hevadanauwauthauwu hadnedasāne: nau naunauthauau Hejavaa
haddugauyāaunāne; henaheee jea henee Naunaudāde hadnenesaunewaude
Heau Hejavaneauthau hadnethade.

Nau, naune, have Elisabeth, jea wauwu henesaunede hadneahede wauwu
dauvadavehahenede: nau nuu nanedaudauchunethe hesese wauwu hehauwunesaune
neethade.

Hanau Hejavaneauthau vahee neneedede hayauhuhau.

Nau naune, Vahadāde daununānau hahagu Mare, hadnaasauau nuu
hasenehene, nau hautheaun hanaāenauthāde.

Nau Mare hanaāethauwauthede hena hese, nau hanaāehaude
jauaudanenauauwuu nauhauhaunee hesee hedan Juda;

Nau hanaejedāde hedauauwunene Zacharias, nau haenaunenechautha
Elisabeth.

Nau hethauwuu, henee, Elisabeth hāenedauwauthaude daunaunenechuhunith
Mare, dāeyaunaa haenauauwuja hethauwaudauau; nau Elisabeth
haeechauchaunaude Vadanauwauthauwu:

Nau haevasanade, nau hanaāenehede, Hauwunaunane hesānaa, nau
hauwunaunade dāeyaunaa hethauwaudauau.

Nau naudauhuthedasaa nuu, henee henaune Navahadāhewaunāde
hadnedasānau?

Hanau, naune, chauchaunu hanadedaunau haunaunenechauhude
hāenedauwaudauwau naunaudaunuu, dāeyaunaa naunauauwujahede naunaudauau
heee haunauwuthajaude.
